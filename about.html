Our Origins & Future

Kuwait Technical College positions itself as a technical educational institute that values and prioritizes practical knowledge combined with theoretical education. Since our establishment, ktech has endeavored to abide by this commitment and in addition to offering a variety of academic courses – ktech has hosted several workshops, training programs and seminars for students, alumni and members of the community ensuring that students go above and beyond the traditional classroom experience.

Building upon the successes of our technical expertise, positioning and reputation as a leading technical education institute, ktech has now established the Center for Continuing Education (CCE) to further its mission of engagement with the professional community. Our Continued Education courses are designed to stimulate your curiosity, challenge your limits, and support your efforts fostering a spirit of discovery and innovation.

Vision & Mission

The Center for Continuing Education aspires to become the leading technical training institute in Kuwait specializing in the provision of on-demand training offerings for individuals and enterprise.

The CCE aims to realize its vision through a transparent communication process with our stakeholders. To achieve this, CCE will:

  • Conduct thorough needs assessments across various industries at regular intervals
  • Ensure the direct transferability of all skillsets acquired in CCE’s training programs onto the participants’ line of work.
  • Design uniquely tailored training solutions and programs based on the emerging needs of the market.
  • Select high-caliber industry professionals to deliver training programs through different modalities; virtual and in-person
  • Provide a robust online training platform that is accessible to the public with wide-ranging training offerings, an interactive learning environment and professional certifications.
  • Maintain the highest standards of quality through a rigorous quality assurance system in place in which feedback from participants and partners is regularly collected, observed and acted up on

Statement of Intent

At ktech CCE, integrity is a way of life. We practice the highest standards of fairness, and ethical behavior to promote the success of every learner. We empower our learners to better their lives and to contribute positively to the organizations and communities in which they work and live. We believe learning is the source of human progress. It has the power to transform our world from illness to health, from poverty to prosperity, from conflict to peace. Learning has the power to transform our lives, for ourselves, our families and our communities. Regardless of who we are or where we are, learning empowers us to change from and redefine what is possible. We believe access to the best learning is a right, not a privilege; We promote equal opportunity for learning and access to education.

We create an intellectually stimulating environment by providing quality education with a student-centered focus where we prepare today’s generation to succeed in tomorrow’s world. We are passionate about providing training and education to upgrade the occupational skills of individuals and the business and industry they serve. We actively collaborate with secondary schools, colleges and universities to enable students' seamless passage between educational systems.

We maintain a vigil on the changing needs of our students, employers, and the communities and are agile in responding through the process of ongoing assessment, feedback, evaluation, and improvement and provide a meaningful transition opportunity for further success in life.
