Who are we?

ktech's Center for Continuing Education offers a variety of technical trainings across different disciplines. We are responsive and adaptive to the industry training needs. We are passionate about providing high quality training and education to upgrade the occupational skills of individuals and the business and industry they serve. We actively collaborate with qualified and established organizations to better prepare you for the workforce.

Read More
Our Vision

The Center for Continuing Education aspires to become the leading technical training institute in Kuwait specializing in the provision of on-demand training offerings for individuals and enterprise.

Our Mission

The CCE aims to realize its vision through a transparent communication process with our stakeholders

Our Plan

Building upon the successes of our technical expertise, positioning and reputation as a leading technical education institute, ktech has now established the Center for Continuing Education (CCE) to further its mission of engagement with the professional community.
